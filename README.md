# Maker Margin — Etsy fee calculator

A free, single-file calculator that shows Etsy sellers exactly what they keep after listing, transaction, and payment processing fees.

**Live:** https://heydavid.github.io/maker-margin-calculator/

## What it does

Punch in your item price, shipping, and material costs. See:

- Every Etsy fee on the order, line-by-line
- Net profit and margin %
- Breakeven price for your given costs

Optional toggle for Offsite Ads attribution (12% / 15%).

## Fee structure

US sellers, current as of 2025:

- Listing fee: $0.20 per item
- Transaction fee: 6.5% of total sale (item + shipping)
- Payment processing: 3% + $0.25 of total sale
- Offsite Ads (optional): 12% (under $10k/yr) or 15% (over $10k/yr)

Excludes regulatory operating fees, Etsy Plus subscription, and VAT for international orders.

## Stack

One HTML file. No build step, no dependencies, no tracking. Drop it on any static host.
